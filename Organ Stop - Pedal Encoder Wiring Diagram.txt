Wiring Overview

I²C Bus (shared)

All devices share the same I²C bus:
	•	SDA → Teensy SDA (pin 18 on Teensy 4.x)
	•	SCL → Teensy SCL (pin 19 on Teensy 4.x)
	•	VCC → 3.3 V
	•	GND → Common ground

Each MCP23017:
	•	All 16 GPIO pins configured as INPUT_PULLUP
	•	Each switch connects between GPIO pin and GND
	•	No external pull-ups required

OLED Display
	•	Controller: SSD1306
	•	Resolution: 128×64
	•	Address: 0x3C
	•	Connected to same SDA/SCL lines as MCPs

USB
	•	Teensy USB configured as USB MIDI
	•	Single USB cable provides:
	•	power
	•	MIDI communication
